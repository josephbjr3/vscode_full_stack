Shortcut:

duplicate to line below: shift + alt/opt + down
duplicate to line above: shift + alt/opt + up

move a line up: opt + up
move a line down: opt + down

comment line: cmd + /

type in multiple line at once: opt + click the lines you want

backspace a whole line: cmd + backspace

backspace a whole word: opt + backspace

delete a whole line: ctrl + k

highlight line of text to the left: shift + cmd + left
highlight line of text to the right: shift + cmd + right

highlight word to the left: shift + opt + left
highlight word to the right: shift + opt + right
